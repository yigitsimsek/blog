First Thoughts

January 2024

This is my first blog post. I've been thinking about starting a blog for a while, and finally decided to just do it.

The hardest part about writing is starting. Once you begin, the words tend to flow more naturally. At least, that's what I'm hoping will happen here.

I plan to write about things I'm learning, projects I'm working on, and ideas that seem worth sharing. The format will be simple and focused on the content rather than fancy design.

Paul Graham's essays have been a big inspiration for this approach. Clear thinking, simple presentation, and useful ideas.

Let's see where this goes.
