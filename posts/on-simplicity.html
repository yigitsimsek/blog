On Simplicity

February 2024

There's something powerful about simplicity. Not the kind that comes from ignorance, but the kind that comes from understanding something so well that you can explain it clearly.

This applies to writing, to code, to design, and to thinking in general. The best solutions are often the simplest ones, but finding them requires going through complexity first.

Einstein supposedly said "Everything should be made as simple as possible, but not simpler." Whether he actually said it or not, the idea is sound.

In building this blog, I chose the simplest approach I could think of: plain HTML, basic CSS, and no unnecessary features. It works, it's fast, and it focuses attention on what matters - the content.

Sometimes the old ways are the best ways.
